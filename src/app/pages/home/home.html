How EscroLink Works

A simple workflow that builds trust between buyers and sellers on any social platform.

1

Seller Creates Transaction

Seller inputs the product details, price, and buyer's phone number. A secure transaction link is created.

2

Buyer Confirms & Pays

Buyer reviews the item summary and pays into EscroLink's secure escrow wallet.

3

Seller Ships the Goods

EscroLink notifies the seller to ship. Tracking information is updated.

4

Buyer Receives & Inspects

Once goods arrive, buyer checks and confirms that it matches what was ordered.

5

Payment Released

EscroLink releases funds to seller. Transaction closes successfully.
